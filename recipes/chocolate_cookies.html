Chocolate Cookies Recipe

This is my favorite chocolate (not chocolate chip) cookie recipe EVER! The ingredients are so so so simple and it takes under an hour to make!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 stick unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
