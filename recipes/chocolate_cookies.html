Chocolate Cookies Recipe

This is my favorite chocolate (not chocolate chip) cookie recipe EVER! The ingredients are so so so simple and it takes under an hour to make!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 stick unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Mix together flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder in a medium bowl.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy in a separate bowl.
  3. Add egg and vanilla extract to wet ingredients.
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients into wet ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  5. Chill in fridge for 15 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  7. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  8. Once cookies have chilled, use a portion scoop to place the dough onto the baking sheet. Space them 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 15 minutes or until crispy on the outside.
  10. Let the cookies cool on the pan for 5 minutes.
  11. Remove cookies from pan and place onto cooling rack to further cool.
  12. Enjoy your delicous chocolate cookies!
