Crepes Recipe

This is my favorite simple crepe recipe! You can customize the filling for it to be savory or sweet!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 large egg
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup milk (or water)
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Heat up your pan until hot.
  3. Add some butter or oil into your pan and swirl all around.
  4. Use a ladle to pour your crepe batter
  5. Swirl crepe around your pan until it evenly coats the bottom.
  6. Once the crepe looks dry to the touch, flip the crepe.
  7. Cook for about 30 seconds, then remove the crepe.
  8. Fill with your favorite fillings! For sweet crepes, I like strawberries, whipped cream, and cinnamon. For savory crepes, I like pizza seasoning and meat.
  9. Enjoy your simple but delicous homemade crepes!
