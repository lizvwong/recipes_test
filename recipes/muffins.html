Muffins Recipe

This is my favorite customizable muffin recipe! It's so simple and easy to make, and you can add whatever mix-ins you like!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 stick unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup milk (or water)
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Your choice of mix-ins

Steps:

  1. Mix together flour, salt, sugar, and baking powder in a medium bowl.
  2. Combine egg, butter, milk, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
  3. Slowly add dry ingredients into wet ingredients. Mix until almost combined.
  4. Add in your mix-ins. Mix until just combined.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  6. Line a muffin tray with muffin liners.
  7. Pour muffin batter into muffin tray until 2/3 full.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the muffins cool on the pan for 10 minutes.
  10. Remove muffins from pan and place onto cooling rack to further cool.
  11. Enjoy your delicous customizable muffins!
